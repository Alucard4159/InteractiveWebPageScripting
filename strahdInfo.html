Strahd Von Zarovich

In life Strahd Von Zarovich was a count, a prince, a solider, and a conqueror. After the death of his father, King Barov, Strahd waged long bloody wars against his family's enemies. He and his army cornered the last of these enemies in a remote mountain valley before slaying them all. Strahd named the valley Barovia, after his deceased father, and was so struck by its scenic beuty that he decided to settle there.
