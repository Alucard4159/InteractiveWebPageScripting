Welcome to the journey into Barovia.


The Idyllic Valley nestled in the Balinok Mountains was a slice of heaven to those who knew of its existence before Strahd's arrival.
The serenity of the place was forever shattered when Strahd led a bloody crusade against the enemies of his family that ended here with the slaughter of hundreds.


Rolling thunderclouds cast a gray pall over the land of Barovia. A deathly stillness hangs over the dark woods, which are partrolled constantly by Strahd's wolves and other servitors.
